The Mobius Field Greg Cole SPECIAL RULES Single Dimension Any capital L with a subscript refers to that land's number on the board. When referring to movement or targeting, Lₛ is the source land, and Lₜ is the target land. Kₓ refers to the number of boards on the board of Lₓ.

When targeting from land L₁ to L₂, if they are on the same board and L₁ ≠ L₂ the range between those lands is the smallest R such that (|L₁ - L₂| - R = 0 mod K). If L₁ = L₂, but the lands are on different boards, the range between those lands is considered to be 1. You can combine these rules to figure out range to different numbered lands on different boards.

In lands with your {presence}, adjacency and range of lands for all actions is determined by the above rule instead of the normal board adjacency. For each {air}, move an invader from target land from target land (Lₛ) to another land on the same board (Lₜ) where
Lₜ = (Lₛ +/- {presence}ₛ mod Kₛ)
You can move up to one {presence} with the invaders. Repeat this power. Lₛ and Lₜ may be on different boards. When you use this power, pick a prime number P >= 3. Invaders in target land Lₛ and another land on the same board Lₜ each do damage to one another where Lₜ exists on the board and
Lₜ . Lₛ = 1 mod P. Lₛ and Lₜ may be on different boards.
