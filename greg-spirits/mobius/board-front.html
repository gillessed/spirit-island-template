The Mobius Field Greg Cole SPECIAL RULES Single Dimension Any capital L with a subscript refers to that land's number on the board. When referring to a land, Lₛ is the source land, and Lₜ is the target land. Kₓ refers to the number of lands on the board of Lₓ. {presence}ₓ refers to the number of your presence in Lₓ.

In lands with your {presence} L, the only adjacent lands to each land L are L + 1 mod K and L - 1 mod K on the same board. This rule is also used to determine range for your powers. For every 2 {air}, move an {explorer}/{town} from target land from target land (Lₛ) to another land on the same board (Lₜ) where
Lₜ = (Lₛ +/- {presence}ₛ mod Kₛ)
You can move up to one {presence} with the invaders. This power can be used to move {city}. Lₛ and Lₜ may be on different boards. Push each invader on target board from Lₛ to Lₛ + 1 mod Kₛ or to Lₛ - 1 mod Kₛ on the same board. (Each invader must move in the same direction) Each invader moved with this power takes a damage for each of your {presence} in the land it was moved to.
