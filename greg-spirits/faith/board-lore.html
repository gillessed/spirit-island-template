Betrayed by Faith in the Spirits The invaders have taken the worship of certain spirits on their own, but the spirits are not their friends, but rather their enemies. They are turned and used against the remaining invaders to purge them from the island. SETUP: Put 2 {presence} on your starting board, one in each of two different lands with invaders. You may place your {presence} after the setup exploration. Begin the game with 1 energy. Play Style: Gains followers and turns them against the invaders.
