Venerable Ancient Passes on Knowledge Some spirits have been on the island for as long as anyone can remember. They may haved waned in power, but are wise an knowledge and can aid their younger brethren to fend off the invaders. SETUP: You have only 1 unique power. During setup, draw 12 minor powers, and 1 major power, and begin the game with them in your hand. You begin the game with all 13 of your {presence} on your board, distributed in any way you like. Your growth options require you to instead return {presence} to your tracks. As you do, you return them to the left-most slot with a value, and the current value of your growth tracks is the left-most uncovered value. Play Style: Supports other spirits with its diversity of powers.
