Greed Tears At the Fabric of Society Greg Cole SPECIAL RULES Distracted by Wealth Invaders in your lands have +2 health.

For each of your lands, if that land has no {explorer}, for each invader, you may pay 1 energy to have that invader not participate in a ravage action. If you spend 4 or more energy this way in a turn, you may gain a power card at the end of that turn. Greed Lingers in the Hearts of the Invaders Whenever an invaders are moved out of one of your lands, you may move up to 1 {presence} with them for each invader moved. Gather up to 1 {explorer}. If target land has {town} and exactly 1 {explorer}, replace 1 {explorer} with 1 {town}. Replace 3 {explorer} with 1 {town}. Replace up to 2 invaders with 1 {city}. If target land has at least two {town}/{city}, destroy 1 {town}. If target land has at least two {town}/{city}, destroy 1 {city}. If target land has at least two {town}/{city}, destroy 1 {town}/{city}. Repeat this power.
