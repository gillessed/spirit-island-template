Oracle of the Constellations Greg Cole SPECIAL RULES Foresight During each spirit phase, you gain an event card. Look at the bottom 2 event cards, pick one and discard the rest. If you are playing with any placed event cards, and you look through the whole deck, remember where the placed card was, reshuffle the deck, and replace the placed event. Karmic Payment During spirit phase, you gain 2 energy that is kept in a separate pool. This energy can only be used to pay for choice events. Invaders' damage in target land cannot be modified this turn by powers, events or fear cards. If they would do extra damage, that damage is then instead done to the invaders after they ravage. Invaders in target land each do +1 damage. Invaders in target land each do +1 damage. Repeat this power. Resolve a gained event card on your board only. Perform all of its effects as listed. When an effect says each player, only do it for you. When an effect says each board, only do it on your board. Resolve a gained event card for all players.
