The Huntsman Long has the Hunstman guided the children of Spirit Island on how to sustain themselves off the fruits of the land. The Hunstman teaches the dahan to coexist with the land, and only take what's necessary using talents and traditions that are passed down generation to generation. SETUP: Put 1 {presence} and 1 {beast} on your starting board in the highest numbered land with 2 {dahan}. Play Style: Forms dahan hunting parties, stronger version of dahan which are removed at the end of a ravage.
