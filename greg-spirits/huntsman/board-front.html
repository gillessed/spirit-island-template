The Huntsman Greg Cole SPECIAL RULES Spirit of the Dahan Your incarna cannot be moved or placed into lands without {dahan}. Dahan Hunting Parties Your spirit can create dahan hunting parties {hunting-party}. These count as {dahan}. They have 3 health and do 3 damage when fighting back in a ravage. After a {hunting-party} fights back in a ravage, it is removed from the game. During ravage, damage should be dealt to {hunting-party} before normal {dahan}. Man's Best Friend {hunting-party} have +1 health and do +1 damage in lands with {beast}. Add a {hunting-party}. Add a {hunting-party}. Add a {hunting-party} for every 3 {dahan}. Push up to 1 {dahan}/{beast}. Push up to 2 {dahan}/{beast}. Push up to 3 {dahan}/{beast}. Push 1 {dahan} and your incarna from target land to a land with no {dahan} and add {dahan} there. If this power moved your incarna to a land, remove 1 {blight} from that land. If this power moved your incarna to a land, gather up to 3 {dahan} into that land.
