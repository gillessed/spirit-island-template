Pillars of Sacred Geometry What do the pillars mean? How do they spell disaster for the invaders? No one knows the mystery behind their alignments. SETUP: Put 2 {presence} on your starting board in a coastal land with a height of 2 and 1 element token of your choice. Put 1 {presence} in a land on your starting board not adjacent to the first with a height of 1 with an element token of your choice. You can choice your element tokens after choosing your first major powers to set aside. Play Style: Moves the pillars around the board by adjusting the heights of the different pillars until they align, and then unleashing big powers when they do.
