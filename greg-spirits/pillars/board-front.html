Pillars of Sacred Geometry Greg Cole SEE RULES CARD For each land with a different height adajcent to target land, you may push 1 {explorer}/{town}/{dahan}. 1 {fear} per {explorer}/{town}/{dahan} moved. If one of your adjacent lands has a height of 8 or more, you may also push {city} with this power. {city} generate 2 {fear} for moving. If you have at least two adjacent lands each with a different height, gain 1 energy. If you have four such lands, instead gain 2 energy. Gather all {presence} from one adjacent land with your {presence} into target land. You may add or remove 1 from the height of this pillar. If you have at least three adjacent lands each with a different height, you may play your saved major power cards now by paying its cost, targeting the same land as this power, regardless of targeting restrictions, but resolve it during the approriate phase In one adjacent land with your {presence}, you may add or remove the cost of the major power played from the height of that land. If that land has {sacred-site}, instead adjust by any number between 0 and the power's cost. If you did not resolve a major power card, instead adjust by 1.
