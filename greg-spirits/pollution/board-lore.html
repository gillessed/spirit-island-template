Pollution Corrupts the Land Pollution is mankind's biproduct and one of its greatest threats. It comes together as a haunting spirit to drive out it creator. SETUP: Put 2 {presence} on your starting board in a land with {blight}, or land #5 if there is no {blight}, and then add 1 {blight} to an adjacent land. Play Style: Insidiously corrupt the land with your {presence} and {blight}, and strangle the invaders with their own waste.
