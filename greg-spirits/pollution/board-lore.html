Pollution Corrupts the Land Snow buildup in mountains has always been treacherous. When it finally hits a breaking point, it tumbles down the slopes, destroying everything in its path. SETUP: Put 3 {presence} on your starting board in a land with {blight}, or land #5 if there is no {blight}. Play Style: Insidiously corrupt the land with your {presence} and {blight}, and strangle the invaders with their own waste.
