Strange Sightings
- Advance Omen Track
- Monster Surge
- Spawn Clues
Spirits may not add {presence} to the board from their track during the next growth phase. Each spirit gets +1 card play during the next turn.
The Sky Is Bleeding
- Advance Omen Track
- Monster Surge
- Spawn Clues
The Spirit with the most energy gains 1 {clue} per spirit in the game, then on each board, add 1 {corruption} to the coastal land with the most invaders.
Corrupted Business
- Advance Omen Track
- Monster Surge
- Spawn Clues
Each spirit forgets a power card. During the next growth phase, if a spirit gains a power card, they may add 1 {corruption} to one of their lands to gain an additional power card.
It Could Be Worse
- Advance Omen Track
- Monster Surge
- Spawn Clues
The spirit with the most {presence} on the board destroys all but one of its {presence}. For the rest of the game, during growth, that spirit may add 2 {destroyed-presence} and 1 {corruption} to one of their lands, and gain +1 energy, in additional to normal growth actions.
