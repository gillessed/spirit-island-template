{reckoning}: Advance doom by 1. ANCIENT ONE When the doom track reaches 0, spawn Cthulhu in the land with the most corruption.
  • Cthulhu is an invader with 6 health per spirit.
  • The land with Cthulhu always matches the ravage. During ravage, it does 6 damage per spirit, and also does 3 damage to each adjacent land.
  • It can be damaged by any power that can damage {town}/{city}. Any effect which would destroy it instead does 3 damage to it.
  • When a land with or adjacent to Cthulhu builds, add {blight} (even if it has no invaders).
  • Cthulhu only heals 1 health per spirit when time passes.
