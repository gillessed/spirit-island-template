Greg Cole CTHULHU Initial doom: 13 RECKONING On each board, add 1 {corruption} to a land with {blight}, and destroy 1 {presence} from each spirit in that land. ANCIENT ONE When the doom track reaches 0, spawn Cthulhu in the land with the most corruption. Cthulhu is an invader with 6 health per spirit. The land with Cthulhu always matches the ravage. It can be damaged by any power that can damage {town}/{city}. Any effect which would destroy it instead does 3 damage to it. When a land with Cthulhu builds, it builds 3 times. During ravage, it does 6 damage per spirit, and also does 3 damage to each adjacent land. Cthulhu only heals 1 health per spirit when time passes.
