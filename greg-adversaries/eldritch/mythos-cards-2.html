Outbreak
{omen} {reckoning} {beast}

The Curtain Rises
{omen} {reckoning} {beast}

- Gain a power card, but can only play 1 power card the next turn.
or
- Return {presence} to their track. Gain 2 energy per card play on their track.
Together We Stand
{omen} {reckoning} {beast}
Each spirit with another spirit's {presence} in one of their lands gains 1 energy and adds {dahan} to one of those lands.

Each spirit without another spirit's {presence} in one of their lands loses an energy and destroys 1 {dahan} in one of their lands.
