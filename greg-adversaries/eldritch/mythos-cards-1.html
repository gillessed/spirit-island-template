Burden of Greed
- Advance Omen Track
- Reckoning
Each spirit may discard any number of power cards in hand, gaining 1 energy per card discarded. Then each spirit must pay 1 energy for every remaining power card in hand.
For Good or For Evil
- Advance Omen Track
- Reckoning
Up to one spirit adds 1 {corruption} per spirit to one of its lands and gains a power card. If no spirit does this, advance doom by 1.
Treacherous Magic
- Advance Omen Track
- Reckoning
Each spirit forgets a spirit-targeting power. Any spirit who forgot a power card this way gains a power card.
Forgotten Knowledge
- Advance Omen Track
- Reckoning
The spirit with the most {clue} discards all {clue}. Spawn 1 {clue} per spirit.
