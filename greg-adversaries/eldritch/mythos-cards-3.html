Hideous Apparitions
- Advance Omen Track
- Reckoning
Each spirit destroys 1 {presence} in lands with invaders. 2 damage to invaders in that land. Spirit may choose to do this again by paying 2 energy and adding {corruption}.
The World Shakes
- Advance Omen Track
- Reckoning
Spirits cannot add {presence} to the board if their {presence} is in a land with 2 or more corruption.

For the rest of the turn, {clue} remove all {corruption} in a land instead of 1.
Everyone Has a Price
- Advance Omen Track
- Reckoning
Every spirit may pay any amount of energy to do that much damage in one of their lands and add {corruption}.
Leave the Rest Behind
- Advance Omen Track
- Reckoning
Each spirit chooses one of their lands with invaders. Push all {presence} from that land. Spawn 1 {clue} in that land.
